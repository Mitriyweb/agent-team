# Voice and Tone Guidelines

Consistent voice and tone ensure a professional and cohesive user experience across all languages.

## 1. Core Voice: "The Expert Guide"

- **Professional**: Authoritative and accurate.
- **Accessible**: Easy to understand, avoiding unnecessary jargon.
- **Empowering**: Focuses on helping the user achieve their goals.

## 2. Tone: "Calm & Efficient"

- **Direct**: Get to the point quickly.
- **Active Voice**: "Click the button" instead of "The button should be clicked."
- **Positive**: Focus on what can be done, rather than just limitations.

## 3. Style Rules

- **Sentence Structure**: Keep sentences short and clear (max 20 words).
- **Gender Neutrality**: Use gender-neutral terms (e.g., "they", "user") to be inclusive.
- **Formatting**: Use bold for UI elements (e.g., **Save**) and code blocks for commands.

## 4. Cultural Nuance (Localization)

- **Adaptation**: Don't just translate words; adapt the tone to be culturally appropriate for the target locale.
- **Puns & Idioms**: Avoid culture-specific humor or idioms that may not translate well.
- **Consistency**: Maintain the same "Expert Guide" voice across all localized content.

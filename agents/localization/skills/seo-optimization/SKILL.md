## Procedure

1. Review the English source documentation for SEO keywords and structure.
2. Provide SEO optimizations for all translations.
3. Coordinate with the tech-writer to ensure optimizations align with the content.
4. Iterate until the source and localized content are fully optimized.

## Gotchas

- Ensure SEO keywords are culturally relevant and not just directly translated.

## Validation loop

The seo-specialist verifies that all documentation is optimized according to the SEO strategy.

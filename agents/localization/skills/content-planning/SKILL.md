## Procedure

1. Define the scope of content to be created or localized.
2. Delegate tasks to the tech-writer, localizer, and seo-specialist.
3. Coordinate the review process and ensure all translations are accurate.
4. Synthesize the results and provide a final summary of the localization effort.

## Gotchas

- Ensure all agents are using the same glossary for consistency.

## Validation loop

The team-lead verifies that the content plan covers all target languages and that the quality of both source and localized content is maintained.
